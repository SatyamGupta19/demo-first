COURCES WE OFFER



  • B.COM

    • A Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) is a three-year undergraduate degree in commerce that prepares students for careers in business, finance, accounting, and more
    • Duration : The B.Com course is three years long, divided into six semesters.
    • Subjects : The course includes subjects like marketing, accounting, entrepreneurship, taxation, and industrial laws.
    • Eligibility : To be eligible for a B.Com, you typically need to have a minimum aggregate score of 50% in 10+2 or an equivalent examination.
    • Integrated courses : Some universities offer integrated B.Com courses, such as B.Com LLB, B.Com MBA, and B.Com CMA.



  • M.COM

    • A Masters of Commerce (M.Com) is a two-year master degree in commerce that prepares students for careers in business, finance, accounting, and more
    • Duration : The M.Com course is three years long, divided into six semesters.
    • Subjects : The course includes subjects like marketing, accounting, entrepreneurship, taxation, and industrial laws.
    • Eligibility : To be eligible for a M.Com, you typically need to have a minimum aggregate score of 50% in 10+2+3 or an Bachelors degree.
    • integrated courses : Some universities offer M.Com courses, such as B.Com LLB, B.Com MBA, and B.Com CMA.



  • BCA

    • The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a three-year undergraduate degree focused on computer science and its applications. Here are some key details
    • Duration : 3 years
    • Eligibility : Minimum 50% marks in 10+2 with English as a mandatory subject
    • Admission Process : Based on entrance exams (like CUET, SET, IPU CET) or merit
    • Course Fees : Approximately INR 70,000 to INR 3,00,000,
    • Subjects Covered : Programming Languages, Web Development, Database Management Systems (DBMS), Network Administration, Data Structures and Algorithms, Cloud Computing, and Virtualization
    • Salary Expectations :The average starting salary for BCA graduates ranges from INR 3 to 9 lakhs per annum, depending on the role and company
